Wild life

While on vacation in Uganda, you will have the opportunity to go to some of the renown educational centers"zoos" and national parks where you will cross paths with the rarerest animal species like;

  1. The mountain gorilla in Bwindi Impenetrable forest
  2. Uganda kob in lake Mburo National Park
  3. African Elephant in Uganda's nature reserves.

Culture

Uganda is greatly endowed with rich cultures and on arrival, each tourist is given a brochure about the various cultures from which one can choose which cultures interest them the most and which ones they would love to visit.

Ugandan cuisine

Tourists will have a chance to dine at the most prestigious hotels where they will eat meals prepared by the best chefs in Uganda. this will be a time to try out the various local cuisines that Uganda has to offer for example the infamous "kikomando" and "luwombo" which is the pride of the Baganda.

Accommodation

Jumbo Tourists Uganda has a vast connection to the most visited hotels in all regions of Uganda and therefore sleeping arrangements can be made depending on which region one chooses to visit.

Scenery

Uganda has some of the most breath taking views in Africa for example Murchison falls, lake Bunyonyi with its many islands and many more.Uganda is indeed a site for sore eyes.
